How to Play:

Level up by clearing rows

Fill a row to clear it.

Rotate clockwise: A

Rotate counter-clockwise: S

Speed Down: Down Arrow

Move Left: Left Arrow

Move Right: Right Arrow

Slow descent: Up Arrow
