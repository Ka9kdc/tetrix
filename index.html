How to Play:

Random Blocks will fall one at a time.

Falling Blocks can be rotated and moved left and right, so that they can fill in rows.

Fill a row to clear it.

You level up every time you clear 5 rows.

As you increase your level the blocks will start to fall faster.

Game will end when no more blocks can enter the board from the top of the board.

Rotate Clockwise: A

Rotate Counter-Clockwise: S

Move Left: Left Arrow ←

Move Right: Right Arrow →

Slow Down Descent: Up Arrow ↑

Speed Up Descent: Down Arrow ↓

Tetrix

In this version the speed at which the blocks fall will increase as your level increases.

There are seven types of blocks falling down.

In the shape of the letters: L, J, T, S, Z, I and O.

All the blocks can be rotated and moved while falling, so you can control where they are placed.
